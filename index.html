MEHMET VESELI - CEO MEHA RENOVATION

A PROPOS

My name is Mehmet Veseli, founder of MEHA Rénovation.

I started my career in renovation in 2016 at Meti Concept, where I took part in overseeing and managing numerous projects. Always looking to go further, I strengthened my skills with a project manager diploma and a certification in hazardous substances.
‍
By creating MEHA Rénovation, I wanted to build a company that reflects who I am. I like to support my clients from the very first meeting through to the delivery of the project, with clear communication, rigorous follow-up, and a presence at every key stage.
‍
I know that behind every renovation, there are often years of savings, sacrifices, and sometimes a lifelong project. That trust isn't given automatically — it's earned. That's why I personally make sure every project is organized with care, that commitments are kept, and that my clients move forward with peace of mind.
‍
To me, a successful project isn't just about the final result. It's also measured by the trust built throughout the process. That's the vision that guides every project completed by MEHA Rénovation.
